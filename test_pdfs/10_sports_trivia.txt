A: France won the FIFA World Cup in 2018, held in Russia. They defeated Croatia 4-2 in the final. This was France's second World Cup victory, their first being in 1998.

Q: What is a hat-trick in soccer?
A: A hat-trick in soccer is when a single player scores three goals in one game. The term originated from cricket but is now commonly used in soccer and hockey.

Q: What is the marathon distance?
A: The official marathon distance is 26.2 miles or 42.195 kilometers. This distance commemorates the legendary run of the Greek soldier Pheidippides from Marathon to Athens.
